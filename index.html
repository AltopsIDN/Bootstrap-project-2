About Us

IDN E-Report 2025 is an online complaint forum platform developed as a means for the public to submit reports, complaints, and aspirations in an integrated, transparent, and accountable manner. This system allows for faster, more measurable complaint handling, and allows for real-time monitoring by relevant parties.

Service

Complaints Service

Report Monitoring

The report handling process can be monitored in real-time through an integrated system.

Quick Follow Up

Each report is followed up by the relevant agencies efficiently for optimal results.

FAQ

What is E-Report?

Online reporting system to accommodate aspirations and complaints.

Who can use it?

Students, teachers, and parents can create reports.

How to report?

Click the "Add Report" menu and fill in the data completely.

Is the report confidential?

Yes, the reporter's data is protected and will not be published.

How long does it take to process a report?

Usually within 1-3 working days depending on the report category.

Can I monitor the reports?

Of course, the report status can be monitored in real-time.
